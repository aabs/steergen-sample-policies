# Governance

:::rule id="STD-GOV-001" category="process" domain="governance" tags="governance, process, urgent, hotfix, expedited, review"
When an urgent hotfix is required, the team shall follow the expedited review process and document a post-mortem.
:::

:::rule id="STD-GOV-002" category="process" domain="governance" tags="governance, process, document, reviewed, quarterly, standards"
The standards in this document shall be reviewed quarterly by technical leads and updated based on lessons learned.
:::

:::rule id="STD-GOV-003" category="onboarding" domain="governance" tags="governance, onboarding, new, environment, setup, instructions"
When onboarding a new developer, the team shall provide environment setup instructions, access documentation, first PR guidance, and CI/CD requirements.
:::
